Trusted Home Healthcare for Elderly & Bedridden Patients

We understand that your loved ones deserve the best care in the comfort of their own home. Aksraya Health Care provides professional nursing services with registered, experienced nurses who treat every patient with dignity, respect, and compassion.

Our nurses are specially trained in elderly care, post-surgery recovery, chronic illness management, and 24-hour patient monitoring. We serve families across Bangalore who need reliable, trustworthy healthcare support at home.

Comprehensive Home Nursing Care

24-Hour Continuous Care

Round-the-clock nursing supervision for patients requiring constant medical attention and monitoring. Perfect for bedridden patients, post-surgery recovery, or critical care needs.

12-Hour Day/Night Shifts

Flexible shift options (8 AM - 8 PM or 8 PM - 8 AM) for patients who need daytime assistance or nighttime surveillance while family members rest.

Specialized Elderly Care

Dedicated care for senior citizens including medication management, mobility assistance, personal hygiene, vital signs monitoring, and companionship.

Medical Procedures at Home

  • Medication administration and IV therapy
  • Wound dressing and catheter care
  • Injection administration
  • Vital signs monitoring (BP, sugar, oxygen levels)
  • Physiotherapy assistance
  • Feeding tube management

Post-Hospital Care

Transition smoothly from hospital to home with professional nursing support for post-surgery recovery, stroke rehabilitation, or chronic disease management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your nurses registered and qualified?
Yes, all our nurses are registered with valid certifications. We verify credentials and conduct background checks before assignment.
Can we request a specific nurse or change nurses?
Absolutely. We match nurses based on your preferences, and if you're not satisfied, we'll assign a different nurse immediately.
What if our nurse can't come due to emergency?
We always have backup nurses ready. If your assigned nurse is unavailable, we'll send a replacement without any gap in care.
What areas do you cover?
We serve all major areas in Bangalore. For locations outside these areas, please contact us—we may still be able to help.
