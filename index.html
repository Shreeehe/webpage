Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions About Home Nursing in Bangalore

Get answers to common questions about our registered home nursing services, care options, and how we support families across Bangalore.

Are your nurses registered and qualified?
Yes, absolutely. All of our nurses are fully registered with the relevant national nursing authority and hold current, verified qualifications. Every nurse undergoes rigorous background checks, credential verification, and regular training to ensure the highest standard of care. You can request to see our nurses' credentials at any time — we believe transparency is the foundation of trust.
Can we request a specific nurse or change nurses?
Yes, we understand how important consistency and comfort are, especially for ongoing care. You are welcome to request a specific nurse, and we will do our best to accommodate that preference based on availability. If at any point you feel a change would be beneficial — for any reason — simply contact our care coordinator and we will arrange a seamless transition with no disruption to your care schedule.
What if our nurse can't come due to an emergency?
We have a dedicated on-call backup system in place precisely for situations like this. If your assigned nurse is unable to attend due to an emergency, we will notify you as early as possible and dispatch a qualified replacement nurse. Our goal is zero disruption to your care — patient continuity is our top priority.
What areas do you cover in Bangalore?
We currently provide registered home nursing services across Bangalore and surrounding areas. If you are unsure whether we serve your location, please contact us directly — we are always expanding our coverage and may still be able to help. We aim to have a nurse reach you within hours of booking in most cases.
What types of home nursing services do you offer?
Our nurses are trained to provide a wide range of services including: post-surgical and post-hospitalization care, wound dressing and management, medication administration and management, chronic disease monitoring (diabetes, hypertension, COPD), elderly companion care, physiotherapy support, palliative and end-of-life care, maternity and newborn care, and specialized treatments. Contact us to discuss your specific healthcare needs.
How do I book a home nurse in Bangalore?
Booking is simple and flexible. You can call us directly at +91 831 096 2174, fill out our online enquiry form, or send us a WhatsApp message. A care coordinator will respond promptly to discuss your requirements, confirm availability, and match you with the most suitable nurse. We offer same-day bookings for urgent cases where possible.
How much do your home nursing services cost?
Our pricing depends on the type of care required, duration, and frequency of visits. We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden charges. Home nursing in Bangalore typically ranges from ₹1,599 to ₹2,499 per day depending on the service type. Contact us for a free, personalized quote tailored to your specific care plan.
Is home nursing safe for elderly patients?
Home nursing is widely considered one of the safest and most effective options for elderly care. Being in a familiar home environment supports faster recovery, reduces hospital-acquired infection risk, and improves mental wellbeing. Our nurses are specifically trained in elderly care, fall prevention, safe mobility assistance, and medication management for seniors.
Do your nurses carry medical equipment?
Yes. Our nurses arrive fully equipped with professional medical supplies including blood pressure monitors, glucometers, wound care kits, oxygen saturation monitors, and other tools relevant to the individual care plan. For specialized equipment, we coordinate in advance to ensure everything needed is available at your home.
Are your services available on weekends and public holidays?
Yes, we operate 7 days a week including weekends and public holidays. We understand that health needs do not follow a business schedule — our team is available to support you whenever you need us, 24 hours a day.
How do I know I can trust the nurse coming into my home?
Patient safety and family peace of mind are our top priorities. All nurses are thoroughly vetted through identity verification, criminal background checks, reference checks, and professional registration validation before joining our team. You will always receive your nurse's name and photo before their first visit, and we maintain transparent communication throughout the care process.
Can I arrange long-term or regular home nursing care?
Absolutely. We offer flexible care plans from one-time visits to daily or weekly recurring visits. Many of our clients set up regular care schedules for chronic condition management, post-surgery recovery, elderly support, or companion care. Our coordinator will work with you to design a customized care plan that fits your needs and budget.
